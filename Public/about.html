About

The Cat NFT project is an initiative dedicated to the mascot and uses the Ethereum blockchain to create collectible NFT digital assets. As part of the project, a limited number of 111 unique NFTs depicting Cats were released.

The purchase of Cat NFT is carried out using the Ethereum cryptocurrency. The price of each NFT Cat is 0.001 ETH. Collecting NFT Cat can be an interesting and exciting hobby for fans and its mascot. Also, owners of NFT Cat may have the opportunity to participate in exclusive events and receive bonuses.

NFTs are valuable and collectible assets that can be purchased and traded on specialized NFT trading platforms. Owning a Cat NFT gives the owner a special status and access to certain privileges and opportunities.
