Roadmap

Phase 1: Launch the Project

  • Creation of an online platform for storing and tracking ownership of NFT Cat.
  • Release of unique NFT Cats in the amount of 111 pieces.
  • Spreading information about the project and its benefits for NFT owners.
  • Launch of a Discord server for communication and exchange of ideas between owners of NFT Cats.

Phase 2: Exclusive privelegies

  • Providing NFT Cat owners with exclusive privileges, such as access to exclusive events and activities.
  • Creation of special loyalty programs and bonus rewards for owners of NFT Cat.
  • Conducting virtual meetings and conferences with the participation of senior representatives, where owners of NFT Cats can ask questions and share their ideas.

Phase 3: Additional functional

  • Development and release of a mobile game for managing and tracking NFT Savings.
  • Introduction of personalization functions allowing owners of NFT Cats to customize and adapt their digital assets
  • Development of a mechanism for trading NFT Cats on the platform so that owners can sell or buy assets with other community members.

Phase 4: Partnering

  • Establishing partnerships with other NFT projects and platforms to expand usage opportunities and exchange of NFT Cats.
  • Collaboration with artists and designers to create new and unique NFT Cat designs.
  • Expanding the Cat NFT ecosystem through integration with other blockchain platforms and creating bridges between different blockchains.
